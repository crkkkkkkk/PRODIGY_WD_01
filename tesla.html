Key Features

  • Electric Powertrain:

    All Tesla vehicles are fully electric, powered by high-capacity lithium-ion batteries.

  • Autopilot and Full Self-Driving (FSD):

    Tesla cars come equipped with advanced driver-assistance features, including Autopilot, which provides semi-autonomous driving capabilities. Full Self-Driving (FSD) was an optional software package that aimed to enable fully autonomous driving once regulatory approval was granted

  • Range:

    Tesla vehicles are known for their impressive electric range per charge. The range varies among models, but Tesla has been a leader in extending the distance a car can travel on a single charge.

  • Performance:

    Tesla cars are known for their high acceleration and top speeds. The "Ludicrous Mode" in some models, such as the Model S, enables extremely fast acceleration

  • Energy Efficiency:

    Tesla vehicles are designed to be energy-efficient, contributing to longer battery life and increased overall sustainability.
